THE IMPACT SERIES BY THINK OPAL

Our motivation stems from the necessity to reduce Inequalities, contribute towards the growth of Sustainable cities and communities and promote decent work and economic growth.Social Enterprises & Innovative, Impactful startups are crucial for developing sustainable solutions to social and environmental problems, boosting innovation, and fostering economic growth. They can also serve as models for other organizations looking to implement sustainable enterprise practices. We support enterprises by providing technical & expert support in:

  1. Business Planning & Sustainability Strategy Development
  2. Marketing, Business Development & Sales
  3. Digital marketing & Communication
  4. Stakeholder Engagement
  5. Go to Market Strategy & Implementation
  6. Market Linkages
  7. Partnerships & Collaborations
  8. Crowdfunding Strategies

The series also serves as an opportunity of changemakers in Africa to take ownership of their history, culture & experiences. This will promote a more accurate & nuanced portray of the Africa continent & empower marginalized communities, promote diversity & help preserve African cultural heritage.

Think Opal will run the impact series on our social media platforms every week on Thursday.
